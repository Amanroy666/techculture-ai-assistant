## Engagement Models Explained

### Fixed Price
- Best when requirements are clear and unlikely to change significantly
- We define the full scope, agree on price and timeline upfront
- Change requests outside scope are handled via a change order process

### Time & Material (T&M)
- Billed per sprint (2 weeks) or per hour
- Sprint rate for a 3-person team: ₹3.5L – ₹5L per 2-week sprint
- Hourly rates: ₹3,000 – ₹6,000/hour depending on role seniority
- Best for evolving projects where requirements will change

### Monthly Retainer
- Dedicated team capacity reserved for your project each month
- Predictable cost, flexible work allocation
- Minimum commitment: 3 months
