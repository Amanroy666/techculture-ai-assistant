## AI & ML Questions

**Q: I've heard a lot about AI but I'm not sure where to start. Can you help?**
A: Absolutely — this is exactly what our AI Readiness Assessment is designed for. We'll spend 2–3 weeks understanding your business, your data, and your goals, then give you a concrete roadmap of where AI can help you and what it would take to get there.

---

**Q: Do you build custom AI models or just use off-the-shelf tools?**
A: Both, depending on what's appropriate. For many use cases, fine-tuning existing models or building RAG systems on top of foundation models (like GPT-4 or Gemini) is faster and more cost-effective than training from scratch. We'll always recommend the approach that's best for your situation, not the most technically impressive one.

---

**Q: Do you have experience with LLM applications and RAG systems?**
A: Yes, it's become one of our strongest practice areas over the past two years. We've built production RAG systems for legal firms, healthcare companies, and internal enterprise knowledge bases. We have deep experience with LangChain, LlamaIndex, vector databases (Chroma, Pinecone, FAISS), and LLM APIs from OpenAI, Google, Anthropic, and open-source models via Ollama and Hugging Face.

---

**Q: What about data privacy when using LLMs?**
A: This is a critical concern and we take it seriously. For clients with sensitive data, we prioritize open-source or self-hosted LLM options (like LLaMA 3, Mistral) deployed within their own cloud environment. This means your data never leaves your infrastructure. We also design systems with proper access controls, PII redaction, and audit logging.

---

**Q: Can you help with MLOps — model deployment, monitoring, and pipelines?**
A: Yes. We set up MLOps infrastructure using tools like MLflow for experiment tracking, Airflow for pipeline orchestration, Docker + Kubernetes for scalable deployment, and Prometheus + Grafana for model performance monitoring. We also implement model drift detection so you know when your models need retraining.

A: We use three main pricing models:
1. **Fixed Price**: Best for well-defined projects. You know the cost upfront.
2. **Time & Material**: Best for exploratory or evolving projects. Billed per sprint.
3. **Monthly Retainer**: Best for ongoing work or long-term product development.

We'll recommend the right model based on how clear your requirements are and how much you need flexibility.

## Process & Communication

**Q: What does the project kickoff process look like?**
A: After signing, we schedule a kickoff call within 3 business days. We set up a shared project management space (Notion or Jira), a Slack channel, and agree on communication cadence. You'll have a dedicated project manager as your single point of contact.

---

**Q: How do you handle revisions or scope changes?**
A: For fixed-price projects, we agree on a revision policy upfront (typically 2–3 rounds of revisions included). For scope changes that go beyond the original agreement, we use a change request process with clear cost and timeline impact communicated before proceeding.

---

**Q: Do you sign NDAs?**
A: Yes, always. We sign mutual NDAs before sharing any proprietary information on either side.

---

**Q: What happens after a project is delivered?**
A: All projects include 30 days of free post-launch support. We also offer extended maintenance contracts. For development projects, we provide full source code, documentation, and knowledge transfer sessions.
