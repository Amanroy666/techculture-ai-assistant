# AI Consulting & Strategy Services

## Service Description

Our AI Consulting & Strategy practice is built for organizations that are serious about building AI capabilities — not just experimenting with it. We work with leadership teams, product managers, and engineering heads to shape an AI strategy that is grounded in business reality.

We don't sell you buzzwords. We help you figure out what actually makes sense for your organization — and what doesn't.

---

## What We Offer

### AI Readiness Assessment
A structured 2–3 week engagement where we evaluate your organization's current data infrastructure, team capabilities, technology stack, and business processes. At the end, you get a detailed report with:
- A maturity score across 6 dimensions (data, tooling, talent, process, governance, culture)
- A prioritized list of AI opportunities with ROI estimates
- A 6–12 month AI roadmap with milestones

**Cost**: ₹3,50,000 – ₹6,00,000 / $4,200 – $7,200 depending on org size

---

### AI Strategy & Roadmap Development
For companies that want a multi-year AI strategy, we run 4–6 week engagements involving stakeholder interviews, market benchmarking, use case prioritization, and build-vs-buy analysis. Deliverables include:
- Executive AI strategy presentation
- Prioritized use case backlog
- Technology stack recommendations
- Talent and hiring plan
- Governance and ethics framework

**Cost**: ₹6,00,000 – ₹12,00,000 / $7,200 – $14,500

---

### Proof of Concept (POC) Development
We build fast, focused POCs to validate whether an AI idea will work in practice before full investment. Typical POC timeline: 3–5 weeks. We've run POCs in areas including:
- LLM-powered internal knowledge assistants
- Computer vision quality inspection systems
- Customer churn prediction models
- Real-time fraud detection pipelines
- Recommendation engines for e-commerce

**Cost**: ₹4,00,000 – ₹10,00,000 / $4,800 – $12,000 depending on complexity

---

### GenAI Integration Consulting
We help companies adopt generative AI tools within their existing workflows — responsibly. This includes:
- Evaluating LLM options (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, open-source)
- Building RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) systems on internal knowledge bases
- Designing prompt engineering strategies
- Setting up AI guardrails, content filtering, and evaluation pipelines

---

### AI Vendor Evaluation
We act as an independent advisor when you're choosing between AI platforms, vendors, or SaaS tools. We build evaluation frameworks, run shortlisting workshops, and produce an unbiased recommendation report.

**Typical Timeline**: 2–4 weeks

## Our AI Consulting Process

```
Discovery → Problem Definition → Data Audit → Use Case Design → POC → Validate → Roadmap
```

1. **Discovery Call** (Free): 1-hour session to understand your goals and challenges
2. **Scoping Workshop**: 2–3 day deep dive with your team
3. **Assessment & Analysis**: We do the research and analysis on our end
4. **Findings Presentation**: We walk you through our findings with your stakeholders
5. **Roadmap Handoff**: Final deliverables including roadmap, budget estimates, and recommendations
