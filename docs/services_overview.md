# Techculture.ai – Services Overview

## About Us

Techculture.ai is a technology consulting firm focused on helping businesses embrace the next generation of AI-driven transformation. Founded in 2019, we have worked with startups, mid-size enterprises, and large corporations across industries like fintech, healthcare, e-commerce, logistics, and media.

Our team is a mix of AI researchers, full-stack engineers, data scientists, and business strategists who believe that practical AI — deployed well — creates measurable business value.

---

## Core Service Areas

### 1. AI Consulting & Strategy
We help organizations understand where AI can genuinely add value, which problems are worth solving with ML, and how to build an internal AI culture. This includes readiness assessments, AI roadmap creation, vendor evaluation, and hands-on proof-of-concept development.

### 2. Custom AI & ML Development
We build custom machine learning models, NLP systems, computer vision pipelines, recommendation engines, and GenAI applications tailored to specific business needs. We don't just integrate off-the-shelf tools — we design solutions that fit your data and your workflows.

### 3. Digital Marketing & Growth
Our digital marketing practice blends data analytics with creative strategy. Services include SEO, performance marketing, content strategy, social media management, email automation, and conversion rate optimization — all informed by data.

### 4. Web & App Development
We design and develop modern web applications, mobile apps, and internal tools. Our stack expertise includes React, Next.js, Node.js, Python (FastAPI/Django), Flutter, and cloud-native deployment on AWS, GCP, and Azure.

### 5. Data Engineering & Infrastructure
From data lakes to real-time streaming pipelines, we help businesses build the data infrastructure needed to power both analytics and AI. Services include ETL pipeline development, cloud data warehouse setup, Kafka/Spark-based streaming, and BI dashboards.

### 6. MLOps & AI Infrastructure
We set up robust MLOps pipelines — model versioning, experiment tracking, CI/CD for ML, monitoring and drift detection, and scalable inference infrastructure. Tools we use include MLflow, Kubeflow, Airflow, Docker, and Kubernetes.
